The 4-7-8 Method:
🔵 Inhale through nose — 4 counts
🟡 Hold your breath — 7 counts
🔴 Exhale through mouth — 8 counts
Repeat 4 cycles for maximum effect
🧘 2-Minute Mindfulness
Step 1 — Find stillness
Sit comfortably. Close your eyes or soften your gaze downward.
Step 2 — Breathe naturally
Don't control your breath. Just notice it flowing in and out.
Step 3 — Anchor to now
Notice 5 things you can feel right now — chair, temperature, heartbeat.
Step 4 — Release thoughts
When thoughts arise, acknowledge them and gently return to breath.
Step 5 — Return slowly
Wiggle fingers, open eyes slowly. Carry this peace with you.
